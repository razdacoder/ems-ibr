All Features & Modules

Explore every capability of ExamNova — designed to automate and simplify the entire examination management process.

Timetable Generation

Automatically generate conflict-free examination timetables based on courses, classes, and available time slots.

  • AM/PM period scheduling
  • CBE vs PBE exam type handling
  • Sunday exclusion & date range validation
  • Background processing with progress tracking
  • Department-level and institution-wide views

Hall Distribution

Intelligently distribute class groups across available examination halls based on capacity and constraints.

  • Capacity-aware hall assignment
  • Large class splitting across halls
  • Utilization statistics & optimization grade
  • Distribution CSV export
  • Per-date, per-period configuration

Seat Allocation

Assign specific seats to students using a multi-pass algorithm with strict anti-cheating adjacency constraints.

  • 8-directional adjacency enforcement
  • Pattern-based seating (checkerboard, diagonal)
  • Visual seat grid layout per hall
  • Manual seat assignment for unplaced students
  • Placed vs unplaced summary

Reports & Exports

Generate professional documents for exam halls — attendance sheets, broadsheet timetables, and seat arrangements.

  • DOCX attendance sheets with school branding
  • Excel broadsheet (department & day-period views)
  • CSV timetable export per department
  • ZIP packages of seat arrangements
  • Walk-in rows and signature fields included

Data Management

Complete CRUD and bulk CSV upload support for all system entities — departments, courses, classes, students, and halls.

  • Manual create, edit, delete for all entities
  • Bulk CSV upload with validation
  • ZIP-based multi-file upload support
  • Duplicate detection and conflict handling
  • Upload lock after timetable generation

Background Job Monitor

Track, retry, and manage all long-running tasks with a comprehensive job monitoring dashboard.

  • Real-time progress tracking
  • Filter by status and job type
  • Retry failed jobs with one click
  • Detailed error messages and tracebacks
  • Job history with parameters and results
